↙AIKIDO GDL

sdhsoijfeiwf wejodiew

AIKIDO is a platform for dialogue and exchange between students of art, art history and related degrees, self-taught and emerging creators. AIKIDO consists of sessions designed as a critical accompaniment where researchers and young artists have the opportunity to draw a series of collective responses to exercises and questions that allow them to analyze the singularities of their context, history and production.

Within the sessions, more experienced guest agents shared brief anecdotes that helped to nourish and motivate the workshop participants, always emphasizing the human part of our practice, that is, the errors, disappointments, efforts involved, expectations and difficult moments. It is important to emphasize that it is not about art classes where the attendees obtain the knowledge given by those who taught and managed the project.

The idea is that every person who participates in the program is involved in an honest and vulnerable way in the pursuit of ideas, ethics, models and passions that allow us to produce new enthusiasm within our professional field.

AIKIDO is a project of Lugar Común, Monterrey. Today it is also carried out in Guadalajara through PAOS GDL and in Puebla through the Museo Amparo.

AIKIDO GDL, first season, was guided by Paulina Ascencio, in collaboration with the artist Claudia Cisneros and Lorena Peña Brito.
